Beat egg whites until soft peaks form, gradually add sugar, beat until peaks round over gently; spread on batter in pans. Sprinkle with almonds, 1 Tbsp (15 ml) sugar and cinnamon. Bake at 350 F about 30 minutes.

Assemble

When cool put together with a cream filling. Either use whipped cream or a package of vanilla pudding mix.
