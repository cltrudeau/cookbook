Brussel Sprouts

Makes brussel sprouts edible

Cook time: 5 mins

sides

Ingredients

Brussel sprouts
Olive oil
pinch Salt

Steps

  1. Quarter the brussel sprouts
  2. Lightly coat with olive oil and a bit of salt
  3. Broil on medium until lightly browned
